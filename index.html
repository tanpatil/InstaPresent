InstaPresent

A web-app that creates slideswith just your voice

Created using Google-Cloud, JavaScript, DJango and TensorFlow

InstantPresent uses your computer microphone to listen while you talk.

Based on what you're speaking about, it generates content to appear on your screen in a presentation in real time.

It can retrieve images and graphs, as well as making relevant titles, and summarizing your words into bullet points.
